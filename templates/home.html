About KodeCurrent 2026

KodeCurrent 2026 is a premier hackathon designed to push the boundaries of innovation, bringing student developers together to solve complex real-world challenges. Returning to RGIPT College after a two-year hiatus, this edition promises an unparalleled platform for creativity, collaboration, and skill-building. This hackathon is not just a competition—it is an immersive learning experience where participants can experiment with emerging technologies, develop practical solutions, and gain hands-on exposure to professional coding practices. Whether you are a novice coder eager to learn or an experienced developer ready to innovate, KodeCurrent 2026 offers an environment to grow, connect, and showcase your talent. Students will have the opportunity to work in teams, tackle diverse problem statements, and turn ideas into tangible solutions, all while learning from mentors and industry experts. Gear up for KodeCurrent 2026—where ideas meet execution, and every student has the chance to make a real impact.
